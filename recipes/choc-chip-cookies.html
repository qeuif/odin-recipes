Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This Chocolate Chip Cookie recipe combines cocoa powder and chocolate chips for rich, chewy cookies with a double chocolate flavor. It's an easy and delicious treat for chocolate lovers!

Ingredients

  • 1 and a half cups white sugar
  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • two-third cup cocoa powder
  • three-quarter teaspoon baking soda
  • one-quarter teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • half cup chopped walnets (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 175 degrees C (350 degrees F).
  2. Beat sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Combine flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in another bowl; stir into butter mixture until well blended.
  4. Mix in chocolate chips and walnuts. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven just until set, 8 to 10 minutes. Cool slightly on the cookie sheets before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
  6. Enjoy!
