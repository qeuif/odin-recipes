Description

This Strawberry Ice Cream is a simple, no-churn dessert that is packed with fresh strawberry flavor. Made with just a few ingredients, it is smooth, creamy, and easy to prepareâ€”perfect for when you want a homemade treat without the fuss.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed fresh strawberries
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • one-quarter teaspoon salt
  • 2 drops red food colouring (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine strawberries, milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, salt, and food coloring in a large bowl.
  3. Pour strawberry mixture into the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker; freeze according to the manufacturer's directions.
  4. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm.
  5. Enjoy!
